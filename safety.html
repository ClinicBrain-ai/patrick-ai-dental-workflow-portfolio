Safety

Explicit Boundaries For AI Dental Workflows

Safety is treated as the operating frame for every project: synthetic-first, provenance-aware, human-reviewed, and non-diagnostic.

Intended Use

This portfolio is intended to describe local-only, synthetic-data-first workflow prototypes for organizing dental case materials, drafting reviewable summaries, identifying missing documentation, and evaluating whether generated workflow artifacts remain inside defined safety boundaries.

Public deployment, if ever approved, would still not change this portfolio into a clinical service, real patient intake system, or deployed medical product.

Prohibited Use

  • No patient-facing final medical advice.
  • No automatic second opinion.
  • No treatment recommendation.
  • No diagnosis.
  • No prognosis.
  • No image interpretation.
  • No teledentistry positioning.
  • No clinical validation claims.
  • No regulatory compliance claims.

Principles

Synthetic-First Principle

Prototype examples should use synthetic materials only, with no real patient data and no real PHI.

Human-In-The-Loop Principle

AI-generated summaries, drafts, and review items remain non-final and require manual review.

Non-Diagnostic Workflow Principle

Projects organize information and workflow states without producing clinical decisions.

Provenance-Aware Principle

Workflow artifacts should distinguish source material, generated text, uncertainty, and missing information.

Public Safety Path

The active public site keeps safety evidence inside approved portfolio pages rather than linking to audit packets, private review notes, or artifact markdown.
