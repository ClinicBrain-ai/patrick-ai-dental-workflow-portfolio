# Portfolio Private Review Packet

This packet suggests a local-only review order for a trusted reviewer. It is suitable for private human review, not automatic public release.

## Suggested Local Review Order

1. Open `index.html`.
2. Open `profile.html`.
3. Open `narrative.html`.
4. Open `projects.html`.
5. Open `stack.html`.
6. Open `safety.html`.
7. Open `review.html`.
8. Open `audit.html`.
9. Open `content/artifacts/ARTIFACT_INDEX.md`.
10. Open `content/writing-drafts/WRITING_DRAFTS_INDEX.md`.
11. Open `assets/screenshots/portfolio-overview-thumbnail.html`.

## Artifacts To Inspect

- Phone layer review queue.
- Taiwan Dental Prep packet.
- Human-in-the-loop review checklist.
- Prohibited claims and safe rewrites.

## Questions For Reviewer

- Is the positioning clear for a U.S. hiring/research audience?
- Does the portfolio stay on workflow infrastructure rather than clinical service claims?
- Are all examples clearly synthetic?
- Are any phrases too close to diagnosis, treatment recommendation, prognosis, image interpretation, second opinion, teledentistry, or clinical validation claims?
- Are the blocked actions clear?
- Is the profile concise and useful for private review?
- Is the narrative understandable without extra explanation?

## Decision Options

- Needs revision.
- Safe for private hiring/research sharing.
- Safe to discuss deployment separately.
- Not suitable for external sharing.

Deployment is not approved by this packet. Deployment requires a separate explicit human approval gate.
