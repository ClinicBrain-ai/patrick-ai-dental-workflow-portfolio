# Limited Private Sharing Log Template

## Purpose

Manual local-only log template for limited private sharing.

## Safety Note

Do not store real personal data in this file.

Do not store real email addresses, phone numbers, addresses, employer-sensitive details, confidential reviewer comments, or private company information.

Use anonymized reviewer labels only, such as:

- Reviewer A.
- AI healthcare product contact.
- Research collaborator.
- Startup advisor.
- Hiring-oriented reviewer.

## Repeated Pattern Reminder

- Record only anonymized feedback themes.
- Do not store real reviewer identities, contact details, employers, confidential comments, or private company information.
- Watch for repeated patterns before opening new revision gates.
- Do not open deployment, public sharing, real-user, real-data, or clinical-use gates from a single feedback item.
