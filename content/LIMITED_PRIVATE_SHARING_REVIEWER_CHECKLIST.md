# Limited Private Sharing Reviewer Checklist

Use this concise checklist with trusted reviewers only.

Do not record real reviewer names, real contact details, employer-sensitive details, confidential comments, or private company information in this checklist. Use anonymized reviewer labels only.

## 1. Positioning

- [ ] Is the positioning clear within 30 seconds?
- [ ] Does Patrick sound like a dental workflow infrastructure builder, not an AI dentist?
- [ ] Does the portfolio explain why dental background matters?

## 2. Hiring / Research Fit

- [ ] Does it fit AI healthcare product?
- [ ] Does it fit clinical AI safety / evaluation?
- [ ] Does it fit health data infrastructure?
- [ ] Does it fit human-in-the-loop workflow systems?
- [ ] Does it fit dental AI workflow research?

## 3. Evidence

- [ ] Is the walkthrough convincing?
- [ ] Are the four projects coherent?
- [ ] Are the artifacts concrete enough?
- [ ] Is the Profile page useful for hiring context?

## 4. Safety

- [ ] Any phrase too close to clinical service?
- [ ] Any phrase too close to diagnosis?
- [ ] Any phrase too close to treatment recommendation?
- [ ] Any phrase too close to image interpretation?
- [ ] Any phrase too close to second opinion?
- [ ] Any phrase too close to teledentistry?
- [ ] Any phrase too close to clinical validation?
- [ ] Any phrase implying U.S. licensure or clinical care?

## 5. Communication Quality

- [ ] Is the English natural?
- [ ] Is the structure easy to follow?
- [ ] Is anything repetitive?
- [ ] Is Projects still too crowded?
- [ ] Which page is strongest?
- [ ] Which page is weakest?

## 6. Decision Options

- [ ] `SAFE_FOR_LIMITED_PRIVATE_HIRING_RESEARCH_SHARING`
- [ ] `NEEDS_MINOR_POLISH_BEFORE_PRIVATE_SHARING`
- [ ] `NEEDS_MAJOR_REVISION`
- [ ] `KEEP_INTERNAL_ONLY`
- [ ] `READY_TO_DISCUSS_DEPLOYMENT_SEPARATELY`

`READY_TO_DISCUSS_DEPLOYMENT_SEPARATELY` does not approve deployment. Deployment requires a separate explicit human approval gate.
