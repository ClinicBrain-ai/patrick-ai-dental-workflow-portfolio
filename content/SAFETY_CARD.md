# Safety Card

## Intended Use

This portfolio is intended for limited private hiring, research, AI healthcare product, health data, and workflow-safety conversations.

It demonstrates local-only, synthetic-first, human-in-the-loop AI workflow infrastructure for dental and healthcare-adjacent use cases.

## Prohibited Use

Do not use this portfolio for clinical care, diagnosis, treatment recommendation, prognosis, image interpretation, patient-facing final medical advice, automatic second opinion, teledentistry, clinical validation, regulatory compliance claims, real patient use, or public deployment.

## Synthetic-first Status

All examples are synthetic-first. The portfolio does not include real PHI, real patient records, real imaging files, real clinic details, real contact details, or real patient submissions.

## Human-in-the-loop Boundary

Sensitive outputs must remain draft, reviewable, and manually handled.

AI-assisted content is not final until a human reviewer edits, approves, rejects, or escalates it.

## Non-diagnostic Workflow Principle

The portfolio is about workflow organization and safety boundaries.

It does not diagnose, recommend treatment, estimate prognosis, interpret images, or provide patient-facing final medical advice.

## Provenance-aware Principle

Source-grounded information should remain traceable.

Missing sources and unsupported inference should be marked for manual review.

## Manual Review Requirement

High-risk outputs, missing information, provenance gaps, and boundary-risk wording require manual review.

## Public Deployment Status

`PUBLIC_DEPLOYMENT_NOT_APPROVED`

## Explicit Boundaries

- No real PHI.
- No patient-facing final medical advice.
- No automatic second opinion.
- No treatment recommendation.
- No image interpretation.
- No clinical validation claims.
- No medical device readiness claims.
- No production clinical system claims.
