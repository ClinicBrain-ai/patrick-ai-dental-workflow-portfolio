# Portfolio Review Checklist

Local-only manual audit checklist for Patrick's AI Dental Career & Migration Portfolio.

This checklist exists to protect positioning, safety boundaries, and portfolio clarity before any future public deployment, GitHub Pages discussion, external sharing, hiring review, research review, or portfolio handoff.

Completing this checklist does not mean the project is clinically validated or ready for real patient use. This checklist does not approve deployment. Deployment requires a separate explicit human approval gate.

## Positioning Review

- [ ] The portfolio consistently uses: “Dentist building safe, human-in-the-loop AI workflow infrastructure for dental and healthcare-adjacent use cases.”
- [ ] The portfolio does not frame Patrick as an AI dentist.
- [ ] The portfolio does not frame the work as autonomous clinical AI.
- [ ] The portfolio clearly emphasizes workflow infrastructure, reviewability, provenance, and safety boundaries.
- [ ] The portfolio is understandable to a U.S. hiring / research audience.

## Project Coverage Review

### Dental Case Packet / AI-ready Dental Data Layer

- [ ] Explains structured case organization.
- [ ] Explains source-grounded packet design.
- [ ] Explains missing-information detection.
- [ ] Clearly states non-diagnostic boundaries.

### Clinical AI Review Harness

- [ ] Explains safety evaluation of workflow outputs.
- [ ] Clearly states it is not a medical benchmark.
- [ ] Clearly states it is not clinical validation.

### AI Native Dental Phone Layer

- [ ] Explains review queue and human approval.
- [ ] Clearly states no automatic patient-facing final messages.

### Taiwan Dental Prep / 台灣安心看牙

- [ ] Explains preparation-oriented return-to-Taiwan workflow.
- [ ] Emphasizes organizing materials and questions.
- [ ] Avoids diagnosis, treatment recommendation, price guarantee, timeline guarantee, outcome guarantee, second opinion, or teledentistry framing.

## Artifact Review

- [ ] Artifact index exists and links properly.
- [ ] Synthetic schemas are clearly fictional.
- [ ] Synthetic examples contain no real PHI.
- [ ] Review rubrics avoid clinical validation claims.
- [ ] Safe rewrite examples are clearly marked as boundary examples.
- [ ] Human-in-the-loop checklist emphasizes manual review.
- [ ] Diagrams remain static and synthetic.
- [ ] Artifacts support the portfolio narrative rather than overclaiming product readiness.

## Writing Review

- [ ] Writing drafts explain the system in plain English.
- [ ] Drafts are useful for hiring / research / collaborator audiences.
- [ ] Drafts avoid hype.
- [ ] Drafts avoid “AI will replace dentists” framing.
- [ ] Drafts clearly distinguish what Patrick is building from what he is not building.
- [ ] Taiwan Dental Prep writing remains preparation-oriented and conservative.

## Visual Thumbnail Review

- [ ] Thumbnails are static HTML/CSS only.
- [ ] Thumbnails do not use generated screenshots, automation, binary images, SVG imports, canvas, Mermaid, external assets, or scripts.
- [ ] Thumbnails make the workflow stack easier to understand.
- [ ] Thumbnails do not introduce clinical claims.

## Profile / Narrative Review

- [ ] Profile page does not imply U.S. dental licensure.
- [ ] Profile page does not offer clinical care.
- [ ] Narrative page clearly explains the portfolio storyline.
- [ ] Print-ready profile remains concise and professional.
- [ ] Potential fit areas are cautious and non-exaggerated.
- [ ] Links to projects, artifacts, writing, stack, safety, and contact work.

## Safety Boundary Review

- [ ] No prognosis claims.
- [ ] No image interpretation claims.
- [ ] No automatic second opinion claims.
- [ ] No teledentistry claims.
