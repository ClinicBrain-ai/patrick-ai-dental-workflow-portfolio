# Limited Private Sharing Feedback Collection

## Purpose

This document supports continued limited private sharing of Patrick Chen's local-only English AI Dental Career & Migration Portfolio with trusted hiring, research, startup, and product contacts.

The purpose is to collect anonymized feedback about positioning, clarity, safety boundaries, and hiring/research relevance without adding forms, analytics, tracking, integrations, deployment, or public release.

## Current Sharing Sequence

Recommended limited private sharing path:

1. `handoff.html`
2. `profile.html`
3. `walkthrough.html`

Reviewers may inspect other static pages afterward, but the primary path should stay short and manual.

## Reviewer Types To Ask

Ask a small number of trusted reviewers from categories such as:

- AI healthcare product contact.
- Health data / HITL workflow contact.
- Technical product / startup contact.
- Biomedical informatics / clinical AI research contact.

Do not store real reviewer names, contact details, employer-sensitive details, confidential comments, or personal data in this repository.

## Questions To Ask Reviewers

- Is the positioning understandable within 30 seconds?
- Does Patrick sound like a dentist building AI workflow infrastructure rather than an AI dentist?
- Does the portfolio avoid clinical service claims?
- Which project feels strongest?
- Which wording feels risky or unclear?
- What would make this more credible for AI healthcare teams?
- Would this be suitable for limited hiring/research/networking conversations?

## Feedback Record Format

Use anonymized labels only, such as:

- `Trusted Reviewer C`
- `Trusted Reviewer D`
- `Trusted Reviewer E`

Suggested record fields:

- Reviewer label only.
- Reviewer category.
- Date label.
- Pages reviewed.
- 30-second positioning clarity.
- Strongest page.
- Strongest project.
- Risky or unclear wording.
- Missing context.
- Suggested edits.
- Sharing decision.
- Notes.

## Privacy Reminder

Do not collect or store:

- Real reviewer identity.
- Real reviewer email.
- Real reviewer phone number.
- Real reviewer employer-sensitive information.
- Confidential personal details.
- Private company information.
- Real patient data.
- Real PHI.
- Real imaging files.
- Real clinic details.
- Real addresses.

## Safety Reminder

Feedback collection does not approve:

- Public deployment.
- Clinical use.
- Real patient use.
- Diagnosis.
- Treatment recommendation.
- Prognosis.
- Image interpretation.
- Automatic second opinion positioning.
- Teledentistry service framing.
- Clinical validation claims.
- Medical device readiness claims.
- Production clinical system claims.
- U.S. licensure claims.
- Backend, analytics, forms, integrations, tracking, or database behavior.

Public deployment remains:

`PUBLIC_DEPLOYMENT_NOT_APPROVED`
