## What This Gate Adds To The Portfolio

This gate adds a local-only private human review page and Markdown review packet documents for trusted reviewer handoff. It gives reviewers a review order, review questions, files requiring review, decision options, notes template, and blocked actions reminder.

## What Remains Blocked

- Public deployment.
- GitHub Pages deployment.
- Analytics.
- Contact forms or inputs.
- Real user intake.
- Real patient data, real PHI, real imaging files, real clinic details, real contact details, or realistic hidden patient identifiers.
- Backend services, database integrations, payment integration, external integrations, or automated patient-facing messages.
- Clinical claims, diagnosis, treatment recommendation, prognosis, image interpretation, automatic second opinion, teledentistry, clinical validation claims, medical device readiness claims, production clinical system claims, U.S. licensure claims, or clinical care offers.

## Suggested Next Safe Gate

Manual private human review and revision pass. Any deployment discussion must remain a separate explicit human approval gate.
