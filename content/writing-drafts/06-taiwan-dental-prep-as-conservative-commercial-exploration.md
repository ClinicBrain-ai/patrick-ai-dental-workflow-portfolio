# Taiwan Dental Prep As Conservative Commercial Exploration

> 回台看牙前，先把資料整理清楚。

Taiwan Dental Prep / 台灣安心看牙 is a conservative use case for validating the Dental AI Workflow Safety Stack without crossing clinical boundaries.

The idea is to help overseas Taiwanese organize existing information before returning to Taiwan for dental care.

## What It Helps Organize

The preparation packet can clarify:

- Available documents.
- Missing documents.
- Travel and timing constraints.
- Questions for a Taiwan dentist.
- Uncertainty notes.
- Document reminders.

It does not decide what care someone needs. It helps a person prepare for a better in-person conversation with a Taiwan dentist.

## Why Taiwan Dental Context Matters

Taiwan dental care is often associated with a conservative, natural-tooth-preserving, patient-communication-focused philosophy. A preparation workflow can support that philosophy by helping people bring clearer context and better questions to the appointment.

The product value is in preparation, organization, and communication readiness.

## What This Use Case Is Not

Taiwan Dental Prep is not a diagnosis, second opinion, treatment recommendation, teledentistry service, price guarantee, timeline guarantee, outcome guarantee, prognosis, or image interpretation tool.

It does not replace a Taiwan dentist. It does not provide automatic patient-facing final messages.

## Why This Can Validate The Stack

This use case can validate whether the workflow stack is understandable and valuable:

- Can people organize fragmented documents?
- Can missing information be surfaced clearly?
- Can questions be prepared safely?
- Can outputs remain reviewable and non-diagnostic?
- Can the system stay synthetic-first during portfolio development?

That is a conservative commercial exploration path: test the preparation workflow, not clinical decision-making.
