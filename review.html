Portfolio Review Checklist

Manual Pre-sharing Review

This local-only manual review checklist helps Patrick audit positioning, safety boundaries, and portfolio clarity before any future external sharing, deployment approval discussion, GitHub Pages discussion, hiring review, research review, or portfolio handoff.

Completing this checklist does not mean the project is clinically validated, ready for real patient use, or approved for deployment. Deployment requires a separate explicit human approval gate.

Positioning Review

  • [ ] The portfolio consistently uses: “Dentist building safe, human-in-the-loop AI workflow infrastructure for dental and healthcare-adjacent use cases.”
  • [ ] The portfolio does not frame Patrick as an AI dentist.
  • [ ] The portfolio does not frame the work as autonomous clinical AI.
  • [ ] The portfolio clearly emphasizes workflow infrastructure, reviewability, provenance, and safety boundaries.
  • [ ] The portfolio is understandable to a U.S. hiring / research audience.
