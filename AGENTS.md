# Instructions For Future Coding Agents

Preserve the non-diagnostic positioning of this portfolio.

Patrick Chen should be presented as:

> Dentist building safe, human-in-the-loop AI workflow infrastructure for dental and healthcare-adjacent use cases.

Do not reposition the site as an AI dentist, diagnostic AI product, treatment recommendation product, autonomous second-opinion service, teledentistry service, clinical validation provider, generic AI enthusiast page, or prompt-engineering-only portfolio.

## Required Boundaries

- Do not add a backend.
- Do not add analytics, tracking, pixels, or telemetry.
- Do not add forms that collect user data.
- Do not add email capture.
- Do not add external integrations or external APIs.
- Do not add deployment configuration.
- Do not add GitHub Actions deployment.
- Do not deploy without explicit approval.
- Do not add real patient data.
- Do not add real PHI.
- Do not add medical claims.
- Do not add patient-facing medical service claims.
- Do not add diagnosis language except when clearly listed as prohibited use.
- Do not add treatment recommendation language except when clearly listed as prohibited use.
- Do not add prognosis language except when clearly listed as prohibited use.
- Do not add second-opinion language except when clearly listed as prohibited use.
- Do not add image interpretation language except when clearly listed as prohibited use.

## Content Style

Keep language conservative, infrastructure-focused, safety-focused, and review-oriented.

Use synthetic examples, workflow diagrams, documentation structures, and safety evaluation concepts rather than clinical output examples.
