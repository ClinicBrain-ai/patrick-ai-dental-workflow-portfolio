Dental AI Workflow Safety Stack

Static synthetic diagram for portfolio review. No runtime code, no real data, and no clinical use.

Layer 1

Case Packet

Organize synthetic sources, missing information, concerns, constraints, and provenance.

Layer 2

Review Harness

Check draft workflow outputs for boundary language, missing sources, and prohibited claims.

Layer 3

Human Review

Route outputs to manual review, edit, escalation, rejection, or archival.

Layer 4

Manual Use

Only human-approved, non-final workflow material may be handled manually outside the prototype.

Boundary: no diagnosis, treatment recommendation, prognosis, image interpretation, automatic second opinion, teledentistry, clinical validation, or automatic patient-facing final message.
