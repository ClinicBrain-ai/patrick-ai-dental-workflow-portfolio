Project 04

Taiwan Dental Prep / 台灣安心看牙

A conservative return-to-Taiwan dental preparation concept for organizing materials, missing documents, travel questions, and questions for an in-person dentist appointment.

Taiwan Dental Prep is presented here as a synthetic, preparation-oriented workflow concept. It is not a public service, not a second-opinion service, and not a treatment recommendation service.
Dental Case Packet Review Harness Phone Layer Taiwan Dental Prep End-to-End Walkthrough

Project Detail

Problem

Overseas Taiwanese adults may have scattered records, uncertain document readiness, and practical travel questions before visiting a Taiwan dentist.

Why this matters

Better preparation can help a person organize materials and ask clearer questions during an in-person appointment with a qualified dentist.

What this project does

It models a synthetic, human-reviewed preparation packet with document inventory, missing materials, timing questions, and dentist-facing question prompts.

What this project does NOT do

It does not diagnose, recommend treatment, provide a second opinion, guarantee prices, guarantee timelines, interpret images, collect patient records, or provide teledentistry.

Current prototype status

Static portfolio concept with fictional preparation fields; no booking flow, payment, messaging, calendar, email, or clinic integration.

Safety Boundary

The project is limited to preparation and organization. It models how a person could bring better materials and questions to an in-person dentist appointment, without replacing clinical evaluation or offering patient-facing dental advice.

Human-In-The-Loop Workflow

A human reviews the preparation packet, removes unsafe wording, confirms missing information, and keeps all clinical discussion with the treating dentist.

Portfolio Value

This project shows how Patrick can translate dental workflow context, migration planning, and AI workflow safety into a conservative commercial exploration concept.
